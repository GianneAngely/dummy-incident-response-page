Our Incident Response Process

A proven methodology to manage every stage of the incident lifecycle.

1

Assess

Initial triage to evaluate scope, severity, and impact. Our team quickly determines the nature of the threat and prioritizes response actions based on business criticality.

2

Contain

Immediate action to stop malicious activity and isolate affected systems. We implement containment strategies that prevent lateral movement while preserving evidence for investigation.

3

Remediate

Remove threats, patch vulnerabilities, and strengthen defenses. Our experts eliminate malicious presence and implement security improvements to prevent similar incidents.

4

Recover

Restore operations quickly and securely with minimal downtime. We ensure systems are clean, secure, and ready for normal business operations with enhanced monitoring.

5

Report

Deliver detailed insights, lessons learned, and long-term recommendations. Comprehensive documentation includes timeline, impact assessment, and strategic security improvements.
